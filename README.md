# 🌿 My Money, My Future — Kenya Personal Finance Tracker

> *"The plans of the diligent lead to profit as surely as haste leads to poverty."*
> — Proverbs 21:5

A faith-rooted, elegantly designed personal finance tracker built for the Kenyan lifestyle and economy. Track your income, manage your budget by category, log daily expenses, and receive biblical encouragement on your journey to financial wellness — all in one beautiful tool.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Real-time salary summary** — Displays your basic salary and take-home pay, with live tracking of total spent and remaining balance
- **8 Nairobi-tailored budget categories** — Pre-loaded with realistic allocations for Housing, Food, Transport, Utilities, Health, Personal Care, Entertainment, and Savings
- **Expense logging form** — Log any expense with a description, amount, category, and date in seconds
- **Live progress bars** — Each category shows budget vs. spent with a colour-coded progress bar that warns you when you're approaching or over budget
- **Doughnut chart** — Visual breakdown of your spending across all categories, powered by Chart.js
- **Smart insights** — Dynamic, personalised tips that update based on your actual spending patterns
- **Biblical encouragements** — Devotional money wisdom from Proverbs, Luke, Malachi, and more, woven into the experience as warm quote cards
- **Persistent storage** — All transactions are saved via localStorage, so your data stays between sessions
- **Clear all button** — Reset your monthly tracker with a single click

## 💰 Budget Allocation Framework

Budgets are distributed across 8 categories guided by the **50/30/20 rule**, adapted for Nairobi's cost of living:

| Category | Recommended Share |
|---|---|
| 🏠 Housing & Rent | ~27% of net |
| 🛒 Food & Groceries | ~23% of net |
| 🚌 Transport | ~11% of net |
| 💡 Utilities & Airtime | ~8% of net |
| 💊 Health | ~6% of net |
| 👗 Personal Care | ~6% of net |
| ☕ Entertainment | ~3% of net |
| 🏦 Savings | ~16% of net |

> The tool is configurable — set your own net income and the budgets adjust to match your situation.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Option 1 — Use the prompt to generate your HTML file

Copy the full prompt provided and paste it into Claude, ChatGPT, or any capable AI assistant. Ask it to output a **single `.html` file**. Save the file to your computer and open it in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).

### Option 2 — If you already have the HTML file

1. Save the file as `finance-tracker.html`
2. Double-click to open in your browser
3. Enter your net take-home pay to calibrate your budgets
4. Start logging expenses right away — no internet connection needed after initial load

### No installation. No signup. No app store. Just open and go. ✅

## 📱 Tips for Kenyan Users

- **M-Shwari / KCB Goal** — Auto-save your savings allocation on payday so it's never tempted away
- **Naivas & Carrefour** — Log grocery runs immediately after checkout to stay honest with your food budget
- **Matatu / Uber / Bolt** — Log transport costs daily; they add up faster than expected
- **NHIF & NSSF** — These are already deducted from your gross pay, so use your net take-home figure when setting up the tracker

## 🔒 Privacy

All your data lives **only on your device** in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts. No cloud. Your finances stay yours.
